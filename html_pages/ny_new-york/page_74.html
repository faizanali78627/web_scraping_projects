Welcome! I’m a wife, mom of 2 boys (and a dog), first generation Indian American, and a psychotherapist specializing in positive psychology. This strengths-based approach is one that focuses more on what helps you thrive rather than solely focusing on what doesn't. A simple example of this can look like cultivating a mindset shift from "I want to stop being so anxious" to "I am working on being calmer". Positive psychology still allows for a (necessary) discussion on past experiences and present fears, but it doesn't keep people stuck in an unproductive venting cycle, which can often happen in session after session.
