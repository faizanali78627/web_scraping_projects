Are you on the brink of divorce? For many couples, life is turned upside down when one partner says they want/need to end the marriage and the other is hoping to save it. It is a mistake for these couples to either jump into the divorce process OR jump into couples therapy without both partners fully on board. Discernment counseling is a short-term, structured process designed to help couples gain clarity as they make a decision for the future of their marriage. The ultimate goal is for couples to decide between three paths: proceed to separation/divorce, postpone the decision, or commit to all-in couples therapy.
